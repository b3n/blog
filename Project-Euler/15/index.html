Problem 15 asks:

Starting in the top left corner of a 2√ó2 grid, there are 6 routes (without backtracking) to the bottom right corner.

How many routes are there through a 20√ó20 grid?

If you know anything about combinations in mathematics this problem is easy to solve without a line of code. You just use the binomial coefficient: $$\binom{40}{20} = \frac{40!}{20!^2}$$
